Search Commands


  /  
Activate the search bar if it's not currently focused.
  .b | !b  
Show|Hide benchmark problems.
  .t | !t  
Show|Hide ticked problems.
  .p | !p  
Show|Hide project problems.
  .o | !o  
Show|Hide todo problems.
  .e | !e  
Show|Hide exiled problems.
  .u | !u  
Show|Hide upgraded problems.
  .d | !d  
Show|Hide downgraded problems.
  .h | h= $hold(,$hold)*  
Show|Hide problems containing $hold, e.g. h=e9 or h=e9,d18.
  !h | h!= $hold(,$hold)*  
Show|Hide problems not containing $hold, e.g. h!=h14 or h!=h14,h18.
  .r | !r $setter  
Show|Hide problems set by $setter.
  .n  
Search setters instead of problems.
  . | = $grade  
Show problems matching $grade, e.g. @v5 or =v5. 
  ..$grade  
Show problems no harder than $grade. 
  $grade..  
Show problems at least as hard as the $grade. 
  .a | a= $ascents  
Show problems with # of $ascents. 
  ..a$ascents  
Show problems with fewer than # $ascents. 
  a$ascents..  
Show problems with at least # $ascents. 
  .s | s= $stars  
Show problems with # of $stars. 
  ..s$stars  
Show problems with fewer than # $stars. 
  s$stars..  
Show problems with at least # $stars.

Parameterized Commands


  ?nuke=tick  
Delete tick from problem, e.g. https://moonZZZZ.zombull.xyz/hard-times?nuke=tick.
  ?page=$number  
Jump to page, e.g. to reset problems index https://moonZZZZ.zombull.xyz/p/all?page=0.
